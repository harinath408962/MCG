About Game

What is Math Calc Game?
Math Calc Game is a fast-paced mathematics challenge where you solve questions across 5 difficulty modes. Your goal is to answer accurately and quickly to score maximum points.
Modes & Number Ranges
  • Easy: 1 – 10
  • Medium: 10 – 100
  • Hard: 100 – 1,000
  • Pro: 1,000 – 10,000
  • Legend: 10,000 – 1,00,000
Points System
  • Easy → +10 points
  • Medium → +25 points
  • Hard → +250 points
  • Pro → +500 points
  • Legend → +1000 points
Daily Puzzle Rules
  • 1 game per day
  • Same questions for all users
  • Total 20 questions (4 from each mode)
  • Fixed time: 3 minutes
  • Skipped questions appear again after completion
Fair Play Warning
If your score per minute is unusually high, a warning will appear:
  • Easy ≥ 45 points/min
  • Medium ≥ 250 points/min
  • Hard ≥ 1000 points/min
  • Pro ≥ 1500 points/min
  • Legend ≥ 2000 points/min
You must confirm whether it was a fair game or if you used a calculator.
